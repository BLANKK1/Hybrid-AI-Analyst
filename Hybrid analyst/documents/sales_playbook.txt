Firmable Sales Playbook — Q1 2024
==================================
Confidential — Internal Use Only

IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE (ICP)
------------------------------
Primary:
- B2B SaaS companies, 50–500 employees
- Revenue operations or growth teams with >2 people
- Currently using manual prospecting or a legacy data vendor (ZoomInfo, Apollo)
- Budget: $5,000–$50,000/year for data infrastructure

Secondary:
- Financial services firms needing compliance-safe B2B data
- Staffing/recruitment agencies
- Management consultancies

RED FLAGS (do not pursue):
- Pure B2C companies
- Companies with <10 employees (cannot support Enterprise)
- Prospects who want one-time data dumps only

COMPETITIVE POSITIONING
------------------------
vs. ZoomInfo:
- Firmable has fresher data (30-day refresh vs. ZoomInfo's 60–90 day)
- Firmable is ~40% cheaper at equivalent tiers
- Weakness: smaller total contact database volume

vs. Apollo:
- Firmable has better API reliability (99.9% vs Apollo's ~98.5%)
- Firmable has stronger technographics
- Weakness: Apollo has a larger free tier that creates pipeline

vs. Clearbit:
- Firmable covers APAC and South Asia significantly better
- Clearbit stronger in North American enterprise logos

OBJECTION HANDLING
-------------------
"Your database is smaller than ZoomInfo."
→ "Smaller but more accurate. Our 30-day refresh cycle means fewer bounced emails and bad dials. Most teams care more about quality than volume."

"We're locked into a ZoomInfo contract."
→ "We can structure an annual deal that starts at your renewal date. Pilot on a department while your ZoomInfo runs out."

"It's too expensive."
→ Offer 14-day trial + move to annual pricing to unlock 15% discount. If still stuck, escalate to manager for a custom pilot at 50% for 3 months.

DEAL STAGES
------------
1. Discovery (target: 1 call, 30 min)
2. Demo (target: 1 call, 45 min — use live data enrichment demo)
3. Technical Review (optional, for Enterprise — involve Solutions Engineer)
4. Proposal + Negotiation
5. Contract + Close

Average sales cycle:
- Starter → Pro upsell: 7 days
- New Pro: 21 days
- New Enterprise: 45–90 days

QUOTA AND COMMISSION
---------------------
- Reps are on 50/50 base/variable split
- Commission: 8% of ARR for new logos, 4% for expansions
- Accelerators: 12% commission for deals >120% of quarterly quota
- Clawback: 100% if customer churns within 90 days
