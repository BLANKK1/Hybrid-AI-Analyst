Firmable Service Level Agreement (SLA) — Summary Document
===========================================================
Version 3.1 | Effective: February 2024

1. UPTIME COMMITMENTS
----------------------
- Starter Plan:    99.5% monthly uptime
- Pro Plan:        99.7% monthly uptime
- Enterprise Plan: 99.9% monthly uptime

Uptime is calculated as: (total minutes - downtime minutes) / total minutes × 100
Scheduled maintenance windows (Sundays 2–4 AM UTC) are excluded from downtime calculations.

2. INCIDENT RESPONSE TIMES
---------------------------
Priority 1 (Platform down, no workaround):
  - Starter:    8 business hours
  - Pro:        4 business hours
  - Enterprise: 1 hour (24/7)

Priority 2 (Major feature degraded):
  - Starter:    2 business days
  - Pro:        1 business day
  - Enterprise: 4 hours

Priority 3 (Minor issue, workaround available):
  - All plans:  3-5 business days

3. SLA CREDITS
---------------
If Firmable fails to meet the uptime SLA in any given month:
- 99.0–99.5% actual uptime: 5% credit of that month's invoice
- 98.0–98.9% actual uptime: 10% credit of that month's invoice
- Below 98.0%:              25% credit of that month's invoice

Credits are applied to the next invoice. Credits are the sole remedy for SLA breaches and are not redeemable for cash. Maximum credit in any month is capped at 25% of that month's invoice.

4. DATA FRESHNESS SLA
----------------------
- B2B Contact Database: refreshed every 30 days
- Technographics data: refreshed every 60 days
- Custom enrichment pipelines (Enterprise): refreshed per contract terms (typically weekly)

5. SUPPORT CHANNELS
--------------------
- Starter:    Email only — support@firmable.com
- Pro:        Email + in-app chat
- Enterprise: Email + chat + dedicated phone line + Slack Connect channel

6. EXCLUSIONS
--------------
SLA does not apply to:
- Force majeure events
- Issues caused by customer's infrastructure
- Third-party API failures outside Firmable's control
- Free trial periods
- Beta features (clearly marked in product)
