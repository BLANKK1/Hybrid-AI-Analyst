Firmable Refund and Cancellation Policy
========================================
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. OVERVIEW
Firmable offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new Starter and Pro plan subscriptions. Enterprise customers are governed by their individual contracts and do not fall under this general refund policy.

2. ELIGIBLE REFUNDS
- Starter Plan: Full refund within 30 days of initial purchase, no questions asked.
- Pro Plan: Full refund within 30 days of initial purchase. Partial refunds (pro-rated) may be issued between day 31 and day 60 at Firmable's discretion.
- Add-on purchases (e.g., Export Credits, CRM Integration Packs): Non-refundable once credits have been consumed or the integration has been activated.
- Custom Data Models: Non-refundable after work has commenced (typically within 5 business days of purchase).

3. CANCELLATION PROCESS
Customers may cancel their subscription at any time through the account settings dashboard under "Billing & Subscriptions". Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. No partial-month refunds are issued for mid-cycle cancellations.

4. EXCEPTIONS
Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:
- Violation of Firmable's Terms of Service
- Fraudulent use of the platform
- Requests made after the eligible refund window
- API overuse or abuse

5. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND
Email billing@firmable.com with your account email and order ID. Processing takes 5-10 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

6. ENTERPRISE CONTRACTS
Enterprise customers should refer to Section 8 of their Master Service Agreement (MSA) for refund and SLA credit terms. Enterprise SLA credits are capped at 10% of the monthly invoice and are applied as account credits, not cash refunds.
